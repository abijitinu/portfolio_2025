Thought Bubble

Ephemeral, Hyper-Local Social Networking

Project Overview

Thought Bubble is a hyper-local social networking app designed to foster ephemeral, anonymous connections. Unlike traditional social media that focuses on permanent records and curated profiles, Thought Bubble emphasizes the "now." Users can "float" thoughts at their current location, creating temporary chat rooms (bubbles) that others nearby can join. The app captures the fleeting nature of city life, with all content disappearing after 24 hours.

Core Philosophy

  • Ephemeral: Everything is temporary. Bubbles burst, chats vanish, and identities shift.
  • Anonymous: No profiles, no followers. Just pure ideas and vibes.
  • Hyper-Local: Connect only with people within a specific radius (e.g., 5 miles), ensuring relevance to the physical environment.

Key Features

Interactive Map Experience

  • Dynamic Visualization with custom-styled MapKit interface.
  • Smart Filtering for specific vibes (e.g., "#Coffee", "#Sunset").
  • Live Updates based on participant activity.

Anonymous Identity System

  • Whimsical Personas (e.g., "Neon Tiger") generated automatically.
  • Identity Shuffle feature for instant persona refreshing.

Ephemeral Chat Rooms

  • Time-Limited conversations with countdowns.
  • Vibe Checks and Intentions tags.
  • Custom reaction system ("Warmth", "Resonate").
