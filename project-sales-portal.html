The Context

A major American multinational consumer goods company needed a robust system to process and analyze sales data from millions of receipts. The existing manual processes were slow and prone to errors, leading to delays in generating actionable consumer insights.

The Brief

The goal was to design a web admin panel for a receipt processing and consumer insights platform. The system needed to automate data extraction, validate receipts using ML, and provide detailed analytics.

Key Requirements

  • B2B Application with multiple user and role-based access
  • Support for Multiple Tenants, Retailers, and Programs
  • Custom Report Generation
  • Advanced Flagging and Error Reporting
  • Subscription Management

The Audience

The platform serves three main user groups:

  • Business Analysts: Who track sales trends and goal achievement.
  • System Admins: Who monitor system health, ML performance, and manage subscriptions.
  • Customer Support (L1): Who resolve user issues and validate flagged receipts.

The Process

We followed an Agile methodology, working closely with data scientists to understand the capabilities and limitations of the ML models.

Key steps included:

  • Stakeholder Interviews: To define the metrics that matter most to business and technical teams.
  • Data Visualization Design: Iterating on dashboard widgets to ensure data is presented clearly and is actionable.
  • Prototyping: Creating high-fidelity mocks for the receipt analysis workflow to optimize for speed and accuracy.

The Solution

Custom Dashboard

The dashboard is divided into two primary sections to cater to different stakeholder needs:

Business Insights

Focuses on goal achievement and delivery metrics. It answers critical questions like "Are we delivering our requirements?" and tracks whether sales are trending up or down.

Technical Insights

Monitors system health and ML performance. It ensures that data extraction and machine learning models are working correctly. All widgets are interactive and respond to applied filters.

Processing Performance

To maximize efficiency, we track upload times and processing peaks. This visualization creates a clear picture of system load throughout the day.

Receipt Analysis

A powerful grid view allowing administrators to filter and manage millions of receipts with sub-second latency.

Detailed Inspection

Administrators can drill down into individual receipts to verify line items, check OCR accuracy, and confirm merchant details.

Process & Validation

The system uses Machine Learning to score every receipt. High-confidence receipts (>90%) are auto-approved, while ambiguous ones are flagged for manual review.

  • Tampering checks
  • Date verification
  • Merchant matching

Fraud Control Dashboard

A dedicated view for monitoring risk. We visualize fraud detection history to spot attack patterns and prioritize the review queue based on risk scores.

Offer Management

Campaign managers can track the status of all promotional offers in one place. The list view provides a quick snapshot of active, scheduled, and expired campaigns, along with their budget utilization.

Ticketing System (L1 Support)

To support the multi-user nature of the portal, we integrated an advanced ticketing system. Support agents have a specialized dashboard to manage incoming queries, escalate issues, and maintain SLAs.

The detail view provides context-aware history, allowing agents to see previous actions and receipts associated with the ticket instantly.

Subscription Management & Scale

Flexible subscription plans allow clients to purchase "chunks" of additional receipt scans as needed. The interface clearly visualizes quota usage and allows for seamless upgrades.

On-Demand Reporting

Stakeholders can generate detailed audit logs and performance reports instantly. This self-serve model reduces the burden on the data team.

The Outcome

The new portal streamlined the receipt verification process, reducing manual intervention by 40%. The ML-powered validation ensured higher data accuracy, while the custom dashboards provided real-time visibility into campaign performance, empowering stakeholders to make faster decisions.
